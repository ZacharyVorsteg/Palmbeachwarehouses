I help businesses find warehouse, industrial, and flex space across Palm Beach County. My service is completely free to tenants — landlords pay my commission when you sign a lease. You get professional representation at zero cost.

What I Do

I specialize exclusively in industrial and warehouse leasing. That focus means I know every landlord, every industrial park, and every building in the county. When you tell me what you need, I don't just search listings — I call property managers directly, check off-market availability, and negotiate terms on your behalf.

Tenant-only representation. I never represent landlords in negotiations. My job is to get you the best space at the best terms — that's it.

How It Works

  1. Tell me what you need — size, budget, timeline, must-haves like dock access or cold storage.
  2. I source options — pulling from listed inventory, off-market spaces, and direct landlord relationships built over years of local deals.
  3. Tour and compare — I schedule tours, walk the spaces with you, and provide honest assessments of each option.
  4. Negotiate your lease — I handle the back-and-forth on rent, TI allowances, lease terms, and renewal options.

Most clients go from first call to signed lease in 2-6 weeks, depending on their timeline.

Service Areas

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to list my space?
Nothing upfront. Our commission is earned only when your space is leased. Standard brokerage commission applies at lease signing.
How long does it take to lease?
In the current Palm Beach market (6.8% industrial vacancy), our average lease-up time is 45 days. Small-bay spaces under 10,000 SF often lease even faster due to ultra-tight 2.3% vacancy.
Do I need an exclusive listing agreement?
We offer both exclusive and co-broke arrangements. Most landlords prefer exclusive because it guarantees our full marketing effort — but we're flexible based on your situation.
How do you vet tenants?
Every inquiry is pre-qualified. We verify business legitimacy, check references, and assess financial capacity before scheduling showings. You only meet serious prospects.
What if my space needs renovation?
We'll give honest feedback on market-rate potential before and after improvements. Many spaces lease as-is in this market.
Can you help with investment sales too?
Yes. We handle both leasing and sales of industrial properties in Palm Beach County. Whether you want to lease, sell, or explore your options, we can advise.

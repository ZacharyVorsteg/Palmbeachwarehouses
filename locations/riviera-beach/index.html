Riviera Beach Industrial Market Overview

Port Adjacent 0.5 Miles to Port
Modern Fleet Dalfen Industrial

Riviera Beach's defining asset is its proximity to the Port of Palm Beach — one of the Southeast's most active container terminals. Businesses in this submarket enjoy 0.5-mile access to containerized freight, cruise port operations, and international cargo. This positions Riviera Beach as the region's dominant distribution hub for import/export logistics, consolidation operations, and international inventory management.

The intersection of I-95 and Riviera Beach's interior logistics nodes creates unmatched accessibility for regional and national distribution. Trucks can reach Miami in 30 minutes and Orlando in 3.5 hours. This corridor hosts FedEx/UPS regional operations and attracts major 3PL providers who leverage the port-to-I-95 gateway for supply chain efficiency.

Dalfen Industrial's modern Riviera Beach portfolio — including Port Authority Distribution Center and Gateway Commerce Park — sets the standard for contemporary logistics infrastructure. These facilities feature 26-foot clear heights, dock-high loading, modern HVAC, and containerized-freight optimized layouts.

Riviera Beach Industrial Areas

  • Port Access Zone (East Federal Highway) - Premium corridor with direct or near-direct Port of Palm Beach access. Dominates containerized freight, international logistics, and port consolidation ($18-24/SF)
  • I-95 Distribution Corridor (West of I-95) - Value-positioned logistics belt with excellent I-95 on/off-ramp access. Attracts FedEx, UPS, and regional LTL carriers ($18-24/SF)
  • Gateway Commerce Zone (Central Riviera Beach) - Mixed-use and flex warehousing for light manufacturing, assembly, and customer-facing operations ($18-24/SF)

Why Tenants Choose Riviera Beach

Businesses seeking serious distribution operations find exceptional value in Riviera Beach's combination of port proximity, I-95 connectivity, and modern Dalfen infrastructure. At $18-24/SF NNN, the pricing reflects strong market fundamentals. The port-to-highway gateway accelerates supply chains for 3PL operators, international trading companies, and sophisticated distribution networks.

Is Riviera Beach Right for You?

If your business involves international freight, containerized cargo, or port-dependent operations, Riviera Beach is the clear choice in Palm Beach County. The Foreign Trade Zone designation offers duty deferral and reduction benefits you won't find in other submarkets. If you need more traditional warehouse or flex space without the logistics premium, I'd point you toward West Palm Beach or Lake Worth for better value.

Local Market Insider: Riviera Beach

Riviera Beach's industrial identity is inseparable from the Port of Palm Beach. The Blue Heron Boulevard corridor serves as the primary logistics artery, connecting port operations to I-95 and the broader regional distribution network. Foreign Trade Zone (FTZ) designation in the port area offers duty deferral and reduction benefits for businesses handling international goods — a tangible cost advantage that no other PBC submarket can match.

Tenants choose Riviera Beach when their supply chain involves international freight, containerized cargo, or port-dependent operations. The 3PL operators and freight forwarders concentrated here create a logistics ecosystem — customs brokers, drayage carriers, and bonded warehouses cluster near the port, making Riviera Beach a one-stop corridor for import/export operations.

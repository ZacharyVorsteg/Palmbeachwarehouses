Why Riviera Beach is Your Distribution Gateway

Port of Palm Beach Advantage

Riviera Beach's defining asset is its proximity to the Port of Palm Beach—one of the Southeast's most active container terminals. Businesses in this submarket enjoy 0.5-mile access to containerized freight, cruise port operations, and international cargo. This positions Riviera Beach as the region's dominant distribution hub for import/export logistics, consolidation operations, and international inventory management.

Strategic I-95 Positioning

The intersection of I-95 and Riviera's interior logistics nodes creates unmatched accessibility for regional and national distribution. Trucks can reach Miami in 30 minutes and Orlando in 3.5 hours. This corridor hosts FedEx/UPS regional operations and attracts major 3PL providers who leverage the port-to-I-95 gateway for supply chain efficiency.

Dalfen Industrial Leadership

Dalfen Industrial's modern Riviera Beach portfolio—including Port Authority Distribution Center and Gateway Commerce Park—sets the standard for contemporary logistics infrastructure. These facilities feature 26-foot clear heights, dock-high loading, modern HVAC, and containerized-freight optimized layouts. Premium positioning attracts blue-chip 3PL operators, international trading companies, and sophisticated distribution networks.

Market Positioning

Riviera Beach offers competitive rents at $18-24/SF NNN for port-adjacent and distribution-optimized facilities. This pricing reflects strong market fundamentals: port proximity, I-95 connectivity, and modern Dalfen infrastructure. Businesses seeking serious distribution operations find exceptional value in Riviera Beach's combination of strategic access and modern logistics facilities.

Key Industrial Submarkets in Riviera Beach

Port Access Zone (East Federal Highway)

Premium corridor featuring Port of Palm Beach-adjacent facilities. Dominates containerized freight, international logistics, and port consolidation. Facilities offer direct or near-direct port access with optimized loading infrastructure.

Pricing: $18-24/SF | Primary Tenant Type: 3PL Operators, International Traders

I-95 Distribution Corridor (West of I-95)

Value-positioned logistics belt capturing regional and national distribution networks. Modern facilities with excellent I-95 on/off-ramp access. Attracts FedEx, UPS, and regional LTL carriers seeking I-95 frontage and interior connectivity.

Pricing: $18-24/SF | Primary Tenant Type: Regional Distributors, LTL Carriers

Gateway Commerce Zone (Central Riviera Beach)

Mixed-use and flex warehousing accommodating light manufacturing, assembly, and customer-facing retail. Positioned between port operations and I-95. Ideal for businesses requiring operational efficiency and retail accessibility.

Pricing: $18-24/SF | Primary Tenant Type: Mixed-Use Operators, Flex Tenants

Riviera Beach Industrial Real Estate FAQs

Why is Riviera Beach ideal for distribution and logistics?

Riviera Beach's defining advantage is Port of Palm Beach proximity—just 0.5 miles from one of the Southeast's most active container terminals. Combined with I-95 access, this creates unmatched efficiency for businesses managing international inventory, containerized freight, and regional distribution. 3PL operators, international traders, and sophisticated distribution networks choose Riviera Beach for the port-to-highway gateway that accelerates supply chains.

What is the typical rent range in Riviera Beach?

Riviera Beach industrial space ranges from $18-24/SF NNN. Market data based on LoopNet and CommercialCafe. Port-adjacent Dalfen facilities and modern distribution centers command this market-competitive range, reflecting the strong fundamentals of port access, I-95 connectivity, and premium logistics infrastructure.

Which types of businesses thrive in Riviera Beach?

Riviera Beach attracts sophisticated supply chain operators: 3PL consolidators, international trading companies, e-commerce distribution centers, container freight handlers, and regional distribution networks. Businesses valuing port access, containerized logistics, and national distribution efficiency choose Riviera Beach. The port proximity also attracts customs brokers, freight forwarders, and import/export specialists.

How does Riviera Beach compare to other Palm Beach County submarkets?

Riviera Beach occupies the highest strategic position in PBC's logistics hierarchy due to its unmatched port adjacency. At $18-24/SF NNN, it's positioned as a strong value for the access and infrastructure provided. Riviera Beach uniquely combines port access, I-95 connectivity, modern Dalfen facilities, and excellent logistics positioning—making it the premier choice for serious distribution and international trade operations.

Jupiter Industrial Market Overview

$14-20 /SF NNN Annual
Tight Inventory Situation
Pine Glades Primary Hub

Jupiter is northern Palm Beach County - a limited-inventory, premium-pricing market where the supply-demand equation heavily favors landlords. Industrial space is scarce, new buildings are modern with latest hurricane codes and A/C infrastructure, and available space moves fast. If you need to be in Jupiter specifically, expect to compete for leases.

Expect to pay $14-20/SF NNN in Jupiter. That's comparable to Boca Raton premium pricing, but more aligned with a constrained northern market. You're paying for: new construction, modern facilities, tight inventory, and distance from central Palm Beach County logistics hubs.

Jupiter Industrial Areas

  • Pine Glades Submarket (Primary Hub) - The center of Jupiter's industrial market. Modern warehouses with excellent infrastructure. This is where 70%+ of Jupiter's industrial inventory is concentrated. Good logistics operations and distribution focus.
  • Federal Highway Corridor - East-side access with good visibility. Retail-industrial mix, smaller space opportunities, good for businesses needing frontage.
  • Military Trail Area - North-south spine with logistics operations. Good central location within Jupiter. Secondary to Pine Glades but steady activity.
  • Jupiter Industrial Park - Institutional-quality facility with modern amenities. Higher end of Jupiter pricing. Professional mix of manufacturing, flex, and service tenants.
  • I-95 Interchange Zone - Direct freeway access. Premium pricing for location. Limited availability, spaces lease quickly.

Why Jupiter Commands Premium Rates

Jupiter's industrial market is fundamentally constrained: limited new development land, tight existing inventory, and strong tenant demand from northern PBC and Martin County businesses. Landlords don't compete on price - they compete on who gets their space. New buildings built to latest hurricane codes with full A/C infrastructure command top-tier rents. Vacancy rates stay low because supply is tight.

The Jupiter Tenant Profile

Jupiter attracts logistics and distribution operations that need northern PBC positioning, regional headquarters and back offices, light manufacturing and assembly operations, and companies seeking modern, code-compliant facilities. If your business must be in Jupiter for customer proximity or operational reasons, budget for premium rents and move fast when something becomes available.

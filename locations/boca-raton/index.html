Boca Raton Industrial Market

Boca Raton's industrial market offers a mix of modern flex buildings, distribution facilities, and light industrial space. The city's southern location provides strategic access to both Palm Beach and Broward County markets, making it an attractive option for businesses serving the broader South Florida region.

The Boca Raton industrial submarket is characterized by well-maintained properties, newer construction, and a business environment that attracts technology, e-commerce, and professional services companies. Key industrial areas are clustered near I-95, Congress Avenue, and the Florida Turnpike interchange.

Popular Boca Raton Industrial Submarkets

  • Congress Avenue Corridor - Mix of flex and light industrial properties
  • Broken Sound / Park Place - Premium Class A flex and office-warehouse
  • Yamato Road Area - Technology-focused industrial parks
  • Glades Road / I-95 - Distribution and logistics facilities
  • Clint Moore Road - Modern flex space developments

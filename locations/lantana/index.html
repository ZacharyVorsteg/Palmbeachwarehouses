Why Lantana Dominates Value Industrial Space

Concentrated Industrial Portfolio

Lantana is a compact industrial market offering focused opportunity for landlords and tenants alike. The concentrated nature of the submarket means specialized market knowledge, stable landlord portfolios, and a defined set of property types. Businesses benefit from less shopping time, faster leasing decisions, and better familiarity with landlord operations and tenant quality.

North Broadway Corridor Dominance

The North Broadway corridor is Lantana's industrial spine, hosting a mix of warehousing, self-storage, light manufacturing, and distribution facilities. This concentration creates secondary tenancy opportunities: a successful operation in one building attracts compatible neighbors and shared-service providers. The corridor is the state's benchmark for value-positioned industrial real estate.

I-95 Strategic Positioning

Lantana's direct I-95 access with immediate on/off-ramps creates unmatched regional connectivity. Miami metro is 30 minutes south, Fort Lauderdale 30 minutes north, and Orlando 3.5 hours west. This positioning makes Lantana the ideal hub for regional distributors managing tight delivery windows, e-commerce fulfillment centers, and LTL carriers serving Southeast markets.

Value-Oriented Pricing

Lantana's $14-20/SF NNN positioning offers the most competitive rates in Southern Palm Beach County, below neighboring Lake Worth ($16-24/SF) and Boynton Beach ($16-22/SF). For startup distributors, emerging 3PL operators, and value-conscious tenants, Lantana delivers modern or well-maintained facilities at prices that accelerate cash flow versus premium markets.

Local Market Insider: Lantana

Lantana occupies a sweet spot between the higher-priced Boynton Beach and Lake Worth markets. The Hypoluxo Road and Lantana Road corridors provide easy I-95 access, and the area's smaller industrial parks keep overhead manageable for growing businesses. You're close enough to serve coastal clients in Manalapan and Hypoluxo but paying interior rents.

Tenants pick Lantana for value without sacrifice. The commute to central Palm Beach County is straightforward via I-95 or Dixie Highway, and you're positioned to serve both southern and central PBC customers. Key Industrial Submarkets in Lantana

North Broadway Industrial Corridor

Lantana's primary industrial spine featuring warehousing, self-storage, and light manufacturing in concentrated clusters. This is where 70% of Lantana's industrial activity occurs. North Broadway provides immediate I-95 access, stable landlord portfolios, and tenant diversification (distribution, storage, manufacturing).

Pricing: $16-20/SF | Primary Tenant Type: Distributors, Self-Storage, Light Mfg

South Federal Highway Value Zone

Secondary industrial corridor offering smaller, divisible spaces at aggressive pricing. South Federal provides I-95 proximity with lower visibility costs. Ideal for emerging operators, startup distributors, and small-business warehousing seeking economic lease rates with maintained infrastructure.

Pricing: $14-18/SF | Primary Tenant Type: Startup Distributors, Small Business

Lantana Road Mixed-Use Zone

Flex warehouse and mixed-use facilities blending industrial, retail, and service tenancies. Lantana Road provides ground-floor visibility and customer accessibility without premium positioning. Ideal for showroom-plus-warehouse operations, fulfillment centers with customer interaction, and split-use concepts.

Pricing: $14-17/SF | Primary Tenant Type: Flex Operators, Mixed-Use

Lantana Industrial Real Estate FAQs

Why is Lantana a good value option for industrial tenants?

Lantana's concentrated industrial market creates efficiency for both landlords and tenants. The North Broadway corridor provides stable, value-positioned facilities with modern infrastructure and I-95 access. For cost-conscious operators seeking Southern Palm Beach County positioning, Lantana delivers competitive pricing and excellent accessibility.

What types of businesses operate in Lantana's industrial market?

Lantana attracts regional distributors, startup logistics operations, self-storage providers, light manufacturers, e-commerce fulfillment centers, and small-business warehousing. The mix reflects Lantana's value positioning and I-95 accessibility. Emerging 3PL operators and regional LTL carriers find exceptional value in Lantana's concentrated portfolio.

How many industrial spaces are available in Lantana?

Lantana's industrial market is concentrated across the North Broadway corridor and South Federal Highway zones, featuring focused market knowledge, stable landlord portfolios, and efficient property evaluation. The compact nature suits tenants seeking streamlined leasing processes with strong market familiarity and accessibility.

How does Lantana's pricing compare to neighboring markets?

Lantana ($14-20/SF NNN) offers the most competitive industrial rates in the area, below Lake Worth ($16-24/SF) and Boynton Beach ($16-22/SF). Compared to premium markets like Boca Raton ($20-28/SF), Lantana delivers significant value. This positions Lantana as the ideal choice for value-conscious operators seeking efficient, modern facilities without premium pricing.

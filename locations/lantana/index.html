Why Lantana Dominates Value Industrial Space

Concentrated Industrial Portfolio

Lantana punches above its weight as a compact market with 17+ industrial listings. This concentration creates efficiency: landlords maintain portfolio at scale, brokers specialize in the submarket, and tenants navigate a defined set of properties. The focused nature of Lantana's industrial market means less shopping, faster decisions, and better familiarity with landlord operations and tenant quality.

North Broadway Corridor Dominance

The North Broadway corridor is Lantana's industrial spine, hosting a mix of warehousing, self-storage, light manufacturing, and distribution facilities. This concentration creates secondary tenancy opportunities: a successful operation in one building attracts compatible neighbors and shared-service providers. The corridor is the state's benchmark for value-positioned industrial real estate.

I-95 Strategic Positioning

Lantana's direct I-95 access with immediate on/off-ramps creates unmatched regional connectivity. Miami metro is 30 minutes south, Fort Lauderdale 30 minutes north, and Orlando 3.5 hours west. This positioning makes Lantana the ideal hub for regional distributors managing tight delivery windows, e-commerce fulfillment centers, and LTL carriers serving Southeast markets.

Value-Oriented Pricing

Lantana's $11-15/SF NNN positioning sits between Lake Worth ($10-14/SF) and Boynton Beach ($11-15/SF), offering the best value-to-accessibility ratio in Southern Palm Beach County. For startup distributors, emerging 3PL operators, and value-conscious tenants, Lantana delivers modern or well-maintained facilities at prices that accelerate cash flow versus premium markets.

Key Industrial Submarkets in Lantana

North Broadway Industrial Corridor

Lantana's primary industrial spine featuring warehousing, self-storage, and light manufacturing in concentrated clusters. This is where 70% of Lantana's industrial activity occurs. North Broadway provides immediate I-95 access, stable landlord portfolios, and tenant diversification (distribution, storage, manufacturing).

Pricing: $12-15/SF | Primary Tenant Type: Distributors, Self-Storage, Light Mfg

South Federal Highway Value Zone

Secondary industrial corridor offering smaller, divisible spaces at aggressive pricing. South Federal provides I-95 proximity with lower visibility costs. Ideal for emerging operators, startup distributors, and small-business warehousing seeking economic lease rates with maintained infrastructure.

Pricing: $11-13/SF | Primary Tenant Type: Startup Distributors, Small Business

Lantana Road Mixed-Use Zone

Flex warehouse and mixed-use facilities blending industrial, retail, and service tenancies. Lantana Road provides ground-floor visibility and customer accessibility without premium positioning. Ideal for showroom-plus-warehouse operations, fulfillment centers with customer interaction, and split-use concepts.

Pricing: $11-12/SF | Primary Tenant Type: Flex Operators, Mixed-Use

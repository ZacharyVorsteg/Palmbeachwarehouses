Delray Beach Industrial Market

Delray Beach's industrial market has grown significantly, particularly along the Congress Avenue and I-95 corridors. The area offers competitive lease rates compared to neighboring Boca Raton while maintaining proximity to major transportation routes.

The city has seen increased demand from e-commerce, logistics, and light manufacturing tenants seeking well-positioned facilities that can serve the broader South Florida market. New developments and renovated properties provide modern amenities at attractive price points.

Popular Delray Beach Industrial Submarkets

  • Congress Avenue Industrial - Primary industrial corridor with diverse options
  • Park of Commerce - Modern flex and office-warehouse facilities
  • Atlantic Avenue West - Light industrial and distribution space
  • Linton Boulevard Area - Mix of flex and traditional warehouse

Why Delray Beach?

Delray Beach Industrial Market Overview

$12-16 /SF NNN Annual
Central PBC Location
I-95 Atlantic & Linton Access

Delray Beach has a smaller industrial footprint than West Palm Beach or Boca Raton, but the space that exists is well-positioned. The main industrial areas run along Congress Avenue and near the I-95 interchanges at Atlantic Avenue and Linton Boulevard.

Lease rates in Delray typically run $16-20/SF NNN - 10-20% lower than West Palm Beach and 15-25% lower than Boca Raton. Most of the inventory is flex and light industrial rather than heavy distribution, making it ideal for small-to-medium businesses.

Delray Beach Industrial Areas

  • Congress Avenue Corridor - Main industrial corridor, mix of flex and traditional warehouse ($16-20/SF)
  • Atlantic Avenue / I-95 - Excellent highway access, light industrial focus, high visibility
  • Linton Boulevard Area - Smaller units 1,000-5,000 SF, office-warehouse combinations
  • Park of Commerce - Modern flex buildings, higher-end finish ($18-24/SF)
  • W. Atlantic near Military - Older buildings, lowest rates in market

Why Choose Delray Beach

Delray Beach offers a central Palm Beach County location with easy access to both West Palm Beach (15-20 min north) and Boca Raton (10-15 min south). It's ideal for businesses that serve the entire county or need to split the difference between northern and southern markets. The downtown revitalization has also improved the workforce pool and dining/retail options for employees.

What to Expect

Delray Beach industrial space tends to turn over less frequently than West Palm Beach - vacancy is lower, so options can be limited at any given time.

About Delray Beach Industrial

Delray Beach has a smaller industrial footprint than West Palm Beach or Boca Raton, but the space that exists is well-positioned. The main industrial areas run along Congress Avenue and near the I-95 interchanges at Atlantic Avenue and Linton Boulevard.

Lease rates in Delray typically run $12-16/SF NNN - competitive with West Palm Beach and lower than most Boca Raton properties. Most of the inventory is flex and light industrial rather than heavy distribution.

Delray Beach Industrial Areas

  • Congress Avenue - Main industrial corridor, mix of flex and traditional warehouse
  • Atlantic Avenue / I-95 - Good highway access, light industrial focus
  • Linton Boulevard Area - Smaller units, office-warehouse combinations
  • Park of Commerce - Modern flex buildings, higher-end finish

West Palm Beach Industrial Market

West Palm Beach serves as the commercial hub of Palm Beach County, offering the region's most diverse selection of industrial and warehouse properties. The city's strategic location provides excellent access to I-95, the Florida Turnpike, and Palm Beach International Airport.

Key industrial corridors include the Australian Avenue area, Military Trail corridor, and the growing Northlake Boulevard submarket. These areas feature a mix of single-tenant warehouses, multi-tenant flex buildings, and distribution facilities serving both local businesses and regional logistics operations.

Popular West Palm Beach Industrial Submarkets

  • Australian Avenue Corridor - Traditional industrial zone with larger warehouse facilities
  • Military Trail Area - Mix of flex space and light industrial
  • Northlake Boulevard - Growing submarket with newer construction
  • 45th Street / I-95 - Distribution-focused with highway access
  • Southern Boulevard Corridor - Diverse property types and sizes

Why West Palm Beach?

  • Largest industrial inventory in Palm Beach County
  • Direct I-95 and Florida Turnpike access
  • Proximity to Palm Beach International Airport
  • Diverse property sizes from 1,000 to 100,000+ SF
  • Competitive lease rates vs. South Florida metros
  • Strong labor pool from surrounding communities

About West Palm Beach Industrial

West Palm Beach has more industrial square footage than any other city in the county. I know every industrial park from Australian Avenue to Northlake - and which landlords negotiate and which don't.

Most of my West Palm Beach leases run $12-18/SF NNN depending on location and building quality. Older buildings near Australian Avenue tend to be on the lower end; newer flex space near Northlake costs more but has better office build-outs.

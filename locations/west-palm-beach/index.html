Local Market Insider: West Palm Beach

West Palm Beach sits at the intersection of I-95 and the Florida Turnpike, giving tenants direct access to both north-south and cross-state routes. Palm Beach International Airport is minutes from most industrial parks, which matters if your business involves air freight or frequent client travel. Downtown's ongoing development boom — including the Brightline station and new office towers — is pushing service businesses westward into industrial corridors, tightening vacancy in parks along Military Trail and Australian Avenue.

Tenants pick West Palm Beach over other PBC markets for inventory depth: more buildings, more size options, more landlords competing for your lease. That competition works in your favor during negotiations. If you need 5,000 SF or 50,000 SF, West Palm Beach has it — most other PBC submarkets don't.

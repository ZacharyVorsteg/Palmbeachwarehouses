Frequently Asked Questions

Is your service really free?

Yes, 100% free. Landlords pay my commission when you sign a warehouse lease. You get professional representation at no cost. Read about how NNN leases work to understand lease structures.

How quickly can I tour properties?

Usually same-week. Once I understand your needs, I can coordinate multiple property tours in a single day to save your time.

What if I need a custom build-out?

I negotiate tenant improvement (TI) allowances with landlords and can connect you with trusted contractors for any build-out work. Learn more in our tenant improvements guide.

Do you handle purchases too?

Yes. Whether you're looking to lease or buy industrial property in Palm Beach County, I can help with both.

What areas do you cover?

All of Palm Beach County including West Palm Beach, Lake Worth, Boynton Beach, Delray Beach, Boca Raton, and surrounding areas.

What size spaces do you handle?

From small 1,000 SF flex units to large 50,000+ SF distribution centers. I work with businesses of all sizes. See our warehouse sizing guide for help choosing.

Do you handle lease negotiation and renewals?

Yes. I negotiate lease terms, renewal options, expansion rights, TI allowances, and rent abatements on your behalf. My goal is a lease structure that protects your business as it grows — not just the lowest starting rate.

Do you work with brokers and investors?

Absolutely. I partner with brokers on co-brokerage deals and work with investors seeking industrial property opportunities in Palm Beach County. If you're in the industry, call me directly at 561-718-6725.

## SALES PROCESS (15 Min to Agreement)

**Step 1: Cold Contact**
- Email or call. Use specific property address. Show you did homework.
- Goal: 15-min call

**Step 2: Consultation Call**
- Ask about space, timeline, current broker, expectations
- Pitch: "We'll market aggressively for 30 days. Here's what you get..."
- Send: Case study + pitch deck + draft listing agreement

**Step 3: Property Walkthrough**
- Take photos (for future marketing)
- Understand space condition, amenities, challenges
- Discuss lease rate expectations
- Get a feel for landlord's personality (are they reasonable?)

**Step 4: Proposal + Closing**
- Customized proposal email (specific marketing plan + timeline)
- Review call (answer questions, handle objections)
- Listing agreement signature (DocuSign or in-person)
- Immediate: Launch marketing

**Timeline**: Day 1 (cold contact) → Day 5 (call) → Day 10 (walkthrough) → Day 15 (agreement signed)

## RED FLAGS (If This Isn't Working)

**By Day 30, if you're seeing**:
- Fewer than 5 qualified prospects
- <20% response rate on emails
- No Facebook leads despite $300 spend
- No scheduled calls

**Action**:
- Audit landing page (CTA too weak?)
- Audit email subject lines (boring?)
- Audit audience targeting (wrong people?)
- Increase outreach volume (maybe 10 cold calls/week isn't enough)
- Adjust message (maybe "30 days to signed lease" overpromises?)

**By Day 60, if you're seeing**:
- 0 signed agreements after 20+ conversations
- Consistent objections you can't overcome

**Action**:
- Revisit pricing (maybe 4.5% is too high for market?)
- Revisit terms (maybe exclusive should be 6 months, not 12?)
- Revisit messaging (maybe "45-day average" isn't realistic for your market?)

  • Full Name

  • Property Size Preferences (square footage)
  • Lease Timeline (when you need space)
  • Any additional messages or inquiries you provide

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit the Site:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Schedule Property Tours: To coordinate times for you to view properties that match your needs

  • CRM Management: To store your information in Trusenda CRM for lead tracking and follow-up communications
  • Marketing: To send you relevant commercial real estate news, market updates, and property listings (with your consent where required)
  • Improve Services: To analyze how you use our Site and improve our website, services, and user experience
  • Business Operations: To maintain records, process transactions, and manage our business
  • Advertising: To show you targeted ads on Facebook and Google based on your interactions with our Site
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that contain information about your browsing activity. They help us remember your preferences and track how you use our Site.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Site to function properly (e.g., security, form submission)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the Site (from Google Analytics)
  • Advertising/Marketing Cookies: Used by Facebook and Google to track conversions and show targeted ads
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices (e.g., navigation preferences)

Tracking Across Websites

Facebook Pixel and Google Ads use cookies to track your browsing activity across different websites. This allows Facebook and Google to show you targeted advertisements based on your interests and behavior. You can see targeted ads from our campaigns on Facebook, Instagram, and Google platforms.

Opt-Out and Browser Settings

You have control over cookies and tracking:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Visit your browser settings to manage or delete cookies.
  • Facebook Opt-Out: You can opt out of Facebook Pixel tracking by visiting Facebook's Ad Preferences or using the "Don't Sell My Data" controls in Facebook's settings.
  • Google Opt-Out: You can opt out of Google Ads personalization by visiting Google's Ad Settings or using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
  • Do Not Track (DNT): If your browser supports DNT signals, you can enable this feature, though we may not honor all DNT requests.

Note: Disabling cookies may impact your ability to use certain features of the Site or affect the functionality of our tracking systems.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it:

  • Contact Information: Retained for the duration of our business relationship and for any applicable legal hold periods (typically 3-7 years for commercial leasing records)
  • Marketing Information: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Cookies and Tracking Data: Retained according to the specific cookie's expiration date

You may request deletion of your data at any time. We will comply with your request subject to legal and business obligations.

7. California Residents (CCPA) Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you additional rights:

CCPA Rights Include:

  • Right to Know: You can request what personal information we collect, use, and disclose about you
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of personal information collected from you
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: You can opt out if we sell your personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

Submitting a CCPA Request

To submit a CCPA request, email us at zach@palmbeachwarehouses.com or call 561-718-6725. Include "CCPA Request" in the subject line. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our Site and services are designed for commercial real estate professionals and business users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at zach@palmbeachwarehouses.com.

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including:

However, no method of data transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You use the Site at your own risk.

### Post 2 Content Structure

1. **Intro** — PBC rent growth ($12→$16/SF NNN, 33% in 5 years), tenant leverage context (vacancy 6.8% up from 4.2%)
2. **Understand Your Leverage** — Size-dependent negotiating power (big-box 8.5-9.8% vacancy vs. small-bay 2.3-3.0%), timing strategies, lease expiry wave (1/3 of leases by end 2027)
3. **7 Lease Terms Worth Negotiating:**
   - Base Rent & Escalations (2.5-3% vs. 4%, $14K savings over 5 years)
   - Free Rent (3-4 months on 5-year, 4-5 months on 7-year, $40K savings)
   - TI Allowances ($5-15/SF, convert unused to free rent)
   - NNN Expense Caps (4% annual cap, right to audit)
   - Lease Term Length (longer = more leverage, early termination at month 36)
   - Renewal Options (fixed-rate vs. capped FMV, avoid naked FMV)
   - Early Termination & Assignment (sublease at 5-15% discount)
4. **Florida Tax Advantage** — Sales tax eliminated Oct 1 2025, $8,800/yr savings on 10K SF
5. **What to Inspect** — Clear height, power, hurricane rating, zoning
6. **Three Common Mistakes** — Negotiating only rent, skipping attorney, not touring competitors
7. **Sample Negotiation Table** — $120K-$130K total savings on 10,000 SF over 5 years

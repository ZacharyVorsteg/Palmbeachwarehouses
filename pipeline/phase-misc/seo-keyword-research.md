## 10 High-Value Long-Tail Keywords

### 1. "warehouse lease negotiation tips florida"

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Intent** | Informational |
| **Suggested Title** | How to Negotiate a Warehouse Lease in Palm Beach County: A Tenant's Guide |
| **Why It Matters** | PBC warehouse rents have doubled in three years ($12/SF → $22-25/SF gross). Tenants are actively searching for leverage. No local CRE site covers negotiation tactics — results are dominated by generic national legal sites (LegalZoom, ContractsCouncil) and one Florida law firm. This positions PBW as a trusted advisor rather than just a listing site. Captures mid-funnel tenants before they engage a broker. Can localize with PBC-specific examples: typical CAM charges, insurance norms, free rent concessions in the current market. |

## Competitive Landscape

**Who dominates national industrial CRE search:**
- LoopNet, CommercialCafe, PropertyShark, Crexi, CityFeet (listing aggregators)
- CBRE, Colliers, Prologis, Cushman & Wakefield (brokerage market reports — mostly paywalled)

**Local competitors:**
- warehousesmarket.com (ComReal) — South Florida market reports, Miami-Dade focused, minimal PBC content
- kenstrends.com — South Florida industrial commentary, opinion format, not SEO-optimized
- cre-sources.com — South Florida CRE news, deal announcements, not educational content

**PBW's strategic advantage:** Nobody is producing regular, SEO-optimized, Palm Beach County-specific industrial warehouse educational content. Every keyword on this list has a local content gap that national aggregators and brokerages don't fill.

## Market Context (for blog authors)

- PBC industrial asking rent: $15.81/SF NNN (up 12% YoY as of Q3 2025)
- Gross rents: $22-25/SF; small-bay premium product: $25-30+/SF
- Vacancy: 7.9% (Q4 2025) — up 70bps YoY from new supply
- Net absorption: positive 301K SF in Q3 2025
- Development pipeline cooling: only 376K SF under construction
- Key corridors: I-95, Florida's Turnpike, Park of Commerce (1,600 acres)
- Major occupiers: Amazon (1.4M SF across 4 facilities), FedEx, 300+ businesses in Park of Commerce
- PBC design wind speed: 175+ mph (Florida Building Code 7th Edition)
- Florida is one of few states that charges sales tax on commercial rent (currently 5.5% state + county surtax)

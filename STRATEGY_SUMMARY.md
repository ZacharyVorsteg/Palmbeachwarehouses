# Two-Sided Marketplace Strategy - Executive Summary
## Palm Beach Warehouses | 90-Day Implementation Plan

---

## THE GOAL

Transform **palmbeachwarehouses.com** from a tenant-only lead funnel into a **two-sided marketplace** where:
- **Tenants** find industrial space
- **Landlords** list their properties
- **Flywheel effect:** More listings attract more tenants; more tenants attract more landlords

**Expected outcome (6 months):** 8–12 active listing agreements, 5–10 leases/month, predictable lead flow for both sides.

## FAQs

**Q: Won't this distract from tenant business?**
A: No. The two sides feed each other. More listings attract more tenants (flywheel). Tenant content/ads continue unchanged. You're adding a parallel revenue stream.

**Q: How do I find time to do consultation calls with landlords?**
A: Build it into your calendar. The email sequences do qualification work upfront (weeds out low-intent). You're talking to 5–10 warm leads/week, not 100s of cold calls. That's < 5 hours/week.

**Q: What if my landlords don't want to do NNN leases?**
A: You can offer both. The pages/forms accept gross and NNN structures. Marketing emphasizes NNN (market-standard) but you stay flexible.

**Q: How do I know this is working?**
A: Track everything. GA4 conversion events, Facebook Ads dashboard, Trusenda CRM. By week 4, you'll have 50+ landlord leads. By week 8, 150+. By week 12, 300+. If numbers are lower, pivot messaging or audience targeting.

**Q: Can I do this with limited budget?**
A: Yes. Start with organic (blog + LinkedIn) in week 1–4. Run ads in week 5+. Even $300/week (Facebook) gets you 40–50 leads/month.

**Q: What if I don't want to manage email myself?**
A: Trusenda/CRE Mail handles it. You write emails once, automation sends them. You just handle calls/follow-up.

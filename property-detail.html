Property Overview

A well-located 11,563 SF industrial building sitting on a 0.72-acre fenced parcel just south of downtown West Palm Beach. Five drive-in doors, 14-ft clear heights, and 16 on-site parking spaces support a range of light industrial and flex uses. IL zoning accommodates warehousing, distribution, assembly, contractor yards, and most service uses.

The property is less than a mile from I-95 access at Belvedere Road, with additional connectivity to Okeechobee Blvd and Dixie Highway. Nearby tenants include auto service, electrical supply, and HVAC contractors, creating a natural fit for operators serving the Palm Beach / Broward corridor.

Building is offered on a 3–5 year NNN basis. Tenant-improvement allowance is negotiable based on term and credit.

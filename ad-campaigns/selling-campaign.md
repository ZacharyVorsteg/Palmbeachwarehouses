## COMMISSION MATH

Each sale closed = 3% of sale price.
Average industrial property in PBC: $1.5M-$5M sale price.

| Sale Price | 3% Commission | Your 70% |

| $500K | $15,000 | $10,500 |
| $1M | $30,000 | $21,000 |
| $2M | $60,000 | $42,000 |
| $3M | $90,000 | $63,000 |
| $5M | $150,000 | $105,000 |

At $3,300/month ad spend:
- Conservative: 1 sale/month avg × $60K commission = **$60,000/month gross**
- Moderate: 2 sales/month × $60K = **$120,000/month gross**
